Recipe Findr

  • You can filter the recipes by adding the following to your search:
    • Time: e.g. 'quick' for (30 minutes max) or 'fast' (45 minutes max)
    • Diet: one of “balanced”, “high-protein”, “high-fiber”, “low-fat”, “low-carb”, “low-sodium”
    • Cuisine Type: The type of cuisine of the recipe. You can use more than one separated by a space e.g. chinese indian
    • Health label: e.g.'peanut-free' or 'low-carb', these can also be combined
  • If you don't get any results, try removing the filter terms as not all recipes will supply us with that information.
